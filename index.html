Our Mission

With over 10 years of detailing experience—including 7 years at one of Canada’s busiest dealerships—we bring dealership-level quality straight to your driveway. At Sorcery Detailing, our mission is simple: deliver top-tier, hassle-free car care with pride, precision, and a personal touch. Every detail matters, and every car gets the VIP treatment.
